About Freestyle Concepts

Founded by Stephen Carelse, Freestyle Concepts has grown from a local Macassar operation into a trusted printing partner for clients across the Western Cape and beyond.

We serve churches, schools, early childhood development centres, sports clubs, families and small businesses. Our client base extends from Macassar and Strand to Grabouw, Stellenbosch, Cape Town and even as far as New Zealand.

Whether it's a single personalised gift or a bulk run of 200+ uniforms, we deliver quality prints with fast turnaround and honest service.

How it works

Simple process, quality results.

Send your design

WhatsApp or email us your design, logo or idea. Most clients bring their own artwork. Need a custom design? We can create one for you.

Get a quote

We confirm the details: quantity, sizes, items and print method, then send you a quote.

We print

Once confirmed, we get to work. Small orders done in 3 days, bulk orders within 7 working days.

Collect or deliver

Pick up from our workshop in Macassar or arrange delivery.
